Game overview:

The idle portion of the game is stage based. Here players gain resources to level up their heroes to be able to fight the stage bosses. The higher a player goes in the stages, the harder the stages get!

Here's a recommended way to play the game:

  1. Fight through the stages
  2. Level up heroes
  3. Fight the stage Boss
  4. Repeat

If you are unable to beat the boss let the game idle for a while so you can accumulate resources to level up your heroes to be strong enough to beat the boss. Then start repeating the listed process again.

The game also features both PVP and PVE aspects that help you gain more resources to level up your heroes.

PVP Aspects:

  • Guilds
  • Arena
  • Elite Arena

PVE Aspects:

  • Daily Dungeons
  • Special Dungeons
  • Infinite Tower

Additionally, there is event content that can be either PVP or PVE. Events are only available for a limited time. So be sure to complete them while they are available. The events are another way of gaining resources to increase the levels of heroes.

To gain more heroes you have to summon them. Heroes are summoned using either summoning tickets or gems. Summoned heroes come in different rarities! The higher the rarity the stronger the hero!

Hero rarities listed from strongest to weakest:

  • High Lord
  • Legendary
  • Unique
  • Epic
  • Rare
  • Normal
  • Extra

The heroes are split into collections that are part of a codex. In the codex, as hero collections are completed stat increases are gained. Stat increases gained from the codex are applied universally. This means that it is applied to a player's team as a whole regardless of what heroes are in the team.

!!!IMPORTANT!!!

Yes, players can spend real money on the game in the shop to speed up their leveling process. However, the game is very generous in that if players are actively playing and participating in events, spending real money is not needed. The game gives out a lot of its in-game currencies as players progress through the game. JUST KEEP PLAYING!

There are many other ways to increase the strength of the heroes.

Here is a list of ways to increase a hero's strength and the resources needed:

  • Hero's Level - Uses Hero EXPs
  • Hero's Breakthrough - Uses Hero Strengthening Stones
  • Hero's Advancement - Requires multiples of the same hero
  • Hero's Stat Bonuses - Uses Elixirs
  • Hero's Potential - Uses Dice
  • Accessories - Uses Rainbow Ore
  • Equipment - Requires specific hero weapon
  • Equipment Level - Uses Refining Stones
  • Equipment Breakthrough- Requires multiples of the same equipment
  • Hero's Costume - Costume Tickets

The game also features pets. Pets are summoned and leveled up in a similar way to the heroes are. Additionally, pets have similar rarities except for "High Lord". The main purpose of pets is to increase stats. The stat increases get better as the pet's level increases. Also, just like with the codex, the stat increases from pets are applied universally regardless of which pet is being used.
